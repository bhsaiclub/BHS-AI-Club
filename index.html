About the club

A welcoming place to learn, build, and lead with AI.

The Bridgeland High School AI Club gives students a place to explore artificial intelligence in a creative, collaborative, and student-driven environment. Members can learn fundamentals, build projects, and grow into confident problem-solvers.

Our mission

Our mission is to make AI education more accessible, engaging, and practical for students. We want members to understand how AI works, think critically about its impact, and apply it to meaningful ideas and real challenges.

Learn deeplyBuild understanding through workshops, demos, and hands-on exploration.
Create boldlyTurn ideas into projects, prototypes, and presentations.
Lead responsiblyUse AI thoughtfully, ethically, and with real-world awareness.

Who this club is for

This club is open to students with all experience levels. Whether you are curious about AI, interested in coding, excited about competitions, or eager to build something useful, there is a place for you here.

  • No prior AI experience required.
  • Great for students interested in coding, design, entrepreneurship, or research.
  • Opportunities to collaborate on team projects and community-facing ideas.
What we do

Hands-on experiences that make AI real.

The club is designed around learning by doing. We focus on activities that help students move from curiosity to confidence through real practice.

01

Workshops

Interactive sessions on AI concepts, prompting, coding, data, and model basics.

02

Project teams

Small groups building apps, tools, experiments, and creative AI-powered ideas.

03

Competitions

Preparation for hackathons, showcases, contests, and school or community demonstrations.

04

Responsible AI

Thoughtful conversations about ethics, impact, fairness, and real-world use.

From the founder

A club built to make AI accessible and inspiring.

I started this club to create a space where students could go beyond hearing about AI and actually build with it. My goal is to help students explore powerful technologies in a way that feels creative, welcoming, and meaningful.

I hope this club becomes a place where beginners feel supported, builders feel challenged, and every member feels empowered to use AI to solve problems and make a positive impact.

Srikar Karri · Founder · Bridgeland High School AI Club

Why this matters

AI is shaping how we learn, work, and solve problems. High school students should have opportunities not only to understand these tools, but also to think critically, create responsibly, and lead confidently.

This club is designed to become a long-term student community that keeps growing year after year, with new officers, new members, and new projects — while staying rooted in the same founding mission.
